I'm Mason Buttner, a quantitative researcher focused on systematic trading
strategies, backtesting methodology, and market microstructure.

My current work centers on intraday mean-reversion in leveraged ETFs — building
causal, look-ahead-free backtests and studying how signals like overnight gap
size and market-wide dislocation drive opening-range-breakout outcomes. The
write-ups on this site document that research in full, from data and universe
construction through the transaction-cost model and bias audit.

### Focus areas

- Systematic strategy design and backtesting
- Market microstructure and intraday price behavior
- Causal / look-ahead-free research methodology
- Python-based data pipelines and statistical validation
